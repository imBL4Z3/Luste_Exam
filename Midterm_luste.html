Municipality Profile

Don Victoriano Chiongbian, formerly known as Don Mariano Marcos, is a landlocked municipality nestled in the coastal province of Misamis Occidental. Located at the foot of Mount Malindang, the municipality is blessed with natural wonders that showcase the Philippines' diverse ecological landscape.

The municipality is known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, including pristine waterfalls, crystal-clear springs, and lush mountain scenery. It serves as a gateway to some of the region's most beautiful ecological destinations and offers visitors a peaceful retreat from urban life.
